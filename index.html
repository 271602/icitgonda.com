ICIT Computer Education

ICIT (Institute of Computer and Information Technology Education Society) was founded in 1998-99. Its sole aim is to upgrade the Computer skills in the poor or the persons who do not afford huge amount in studying Computers.
This organization is registered by the Uttar Pradesh State Government, (Under Society Act).
This organization is founded by Mr. Santosh Kumar Gupta. Mr. Santosh Kumar Gupta started the Computer Centre (in the name of ICIT) in the year 1999 with the singular objective of imparting Computer Education to the Poor Talented students with a small set-up comprising of just two computers.
With his devotion dedication, gradually he gained the faith confidence of students in his centre.
Today,
His centre is one of the best equipped centre in the City. ICIT is also the Authorised Training and Study Centre of National Institute of Electronics & Information Technology (NIELIT), DOEACC Society.
